FAQ

Good questions

Is ChildCare Connect free for families?

Yes. Searching, browsing profiles, and contacting providers is free for families. Optional premium features may come later, but the core experience stays free.

How are providers verified?

Providers share their licensing and credentials when they join, and we review every listing before it goes live. We're building toward integrated background checks as we grow.

What does "founding provider" mean?

Providers who join the waitlist before launch get their first 6 months listed completely free — no card required. You'll be among the first families see when we go live.

When do you launch?

We're starting in Wilmington, then Raleigh and Charlotte. Waitlist members hear first — that's the whole point of the list.
